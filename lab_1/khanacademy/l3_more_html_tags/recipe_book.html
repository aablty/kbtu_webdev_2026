Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Milk 1 3/4 cups
Eggs 2 large
Baking powder 2 tablespoons

Step 1: Mix flour and baking powder in a large bowl.

Step 2: Whisk eggs and milk together in a separate bowl.

Step 3: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until just combined.

Step 4: Heat a griddle and pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.

Step 5: Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 1 pound
Bacon 8 slices
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan cheese 1 cup grated

Step 1: Cook spaghetti according to package directions.

Step 2: Fry bacon until crispy, then chop into pieces.

Step 3: Beat eggs and mix with parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Drain pasta and immediately toss with egg mixture.

Step 5: Add bacon and serve immediately.
